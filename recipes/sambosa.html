How to make delicious sambosa recipe?

This is the sambosa's recipe

Sambos is one of the mose delicious appitizers in the the world. It is known for its crunchy taste and golden color.

It could be filled with cheese, beef, chicken, viggies, or herbs. There are endless fillings that you can add to a sambosa pastries

The ingredients for making beef sambosas are:

  • Fresh Pastires
  • Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Mixed herbs
  • Seasons of choice

How to prepare:

  1. Bake the ground beef and mix the herbs
  2. Flat the pasties
  3. Fill the middle of the pastries with the beef
  4. Wrap the angles as you go by folding each side
  5. Oli fry, oven, or air fry all work fine.
